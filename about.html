Tufts Animal Aid is a student-run organization at Tufts University that pairs volunteer undergraduate students with dogs in the nearby Medford/Somerville area. Our mission is to assist the residents of the Medford/Somerville community while providing stress relief and joy to Tufts students.


Volunteers commit to walking their dog once a week for a semester (September through December, and February through May). We encourage interested Tufts students and Medford/Somerville dog owners to reach out to us for more information!
